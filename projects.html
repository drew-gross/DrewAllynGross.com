Drew's Personal Projects

UpPhoto

UpPhoto is a fast photo uploader for Facebook that works similarly to Dropbox. It was developed by Akash, Christophe, Carlo, and me as en entry in the Facebook Hackathon. UpPhoto won the constest, against 50 other teams, and will let us go to the inter-university competition at Facebook Headquarters in November.

DebateWithMe

DebateWithMe is a work-in-progress website that I created to learn ruby on rails. The purpose of the site is to give people a new way to have debates with each other, and the reason I created the site is to explore a different method of having tree style comments, different from what Hacker News and Reddit do. Source code is available on GitHub.
